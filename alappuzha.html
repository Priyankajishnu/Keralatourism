ALAPPUZHA

The district of Alappuzha has a distinct lifestyle owing to the presence of these water bodies.Interspersed with vast stretches of paddy fields,Alappuzha is also famous for coir making and coir-based industries and was popular among foreign traders as the ‘Venice of the East.’ Renowned for its any boat races, Alappuzha also has beautiful stretched fishing villages and where farming is done below sea level.
